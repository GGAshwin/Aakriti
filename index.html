Aakriti 2023

Aakriti 2023 is a state level inter college techno-cultural fest witnessing a footfall of over 5000+ students for a period of 3 days. It celebrates the spirit of participation and achievement in all its glory. In Sanskrit Aakriti means shape or form. With Aakriti, we aim to bring about feelings of fraternity among today's youth by encouraging the participation of students in a plethora of events. Aakriti provides to innovate, create and amalgamate not only their technical know-how but also their cultural and traditional knowledge. Join us and unleash the best in you!

Date

30, 31 March & 1 April 2023

Venue

Canara Engineering College, Benjanpadavu
